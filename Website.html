OUR MISSION

Shekels Trading Corporation was established in 2018 as a privately held company having an office in Caloocan City. Throughout our history, we have built strong client relationships and have been praised by business owners for our attention to detail, on-time delivery and our ability to provide affordable quality products. We provide products and services that give the best value to our customers. We promote good corporate governance and integrity in all business deals.

OUR VISION

We envision Shekels Trading to be a preferred supplier of contractors and one of the leading suppliers of construction materials in the National Capital Region.
